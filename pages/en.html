Dear international student of PetrSU! Here you can find answers to the following important questions:

  • When is the deadline to submit your documents for visa extension?
  • When do you need to reissue your passport?

We remind you that in accordance with the current legislation of the Russian Federation:

  • You must submit your documents for visa extension at least 20 working days (calendar month)1 before your current visa expires if your passport is valid;
  • To apply for visa extension your passport must be valid for at least 18 months2 from the start date of the next visa;

Find out the answers to these questions in two simple steps.

1 Link to the decree in Russian: http://base.garant.ru/73421575/
2 Link to the Government Regulation in Russian: http://www.garant.ru/hotlaw/federal/88817/
